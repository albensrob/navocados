Our Product

Fresh Avocado

Avocados fall into the category of superfoods, especially super fruit because it contains nutrients that are dense and good for health. All parts of avocado include meat, leaves, seeds, skin, oil, to avocado pulp is said to contain substances that are good for the body, such as antioxidants, antidiabetics, and hypotension.

Frozen Avocado

The frozen avocado that we produce is an avocado that has been peeled and then put into hygienic plastic and frozen. The frozen avocado that we produce is very natural and hygienic. does not use chemicals or other mixtures, so it is very safe for consumption.
